# KOOLA10 UNIVERSE — MASTER BIBLE
Version 1.0  
KOOLA10 Emergent Studio  

============================================================

## 1. CORE WORLD CONCEPT
============================================================
KOOLA10 is a living mythos built around three supernatural anchors:

1. The KOOLA10 Diner — a magical nexus where reality bends.
2. The KNB (Koola10 National Busline) Station — a liminal gateway for new characters.
3. Spiral City — the urban heart of freestyle culture and magical street sports.

Magic manifests through:
- Portals (dimensional doorways)
- Orbz (sentient cameras/judges)
- Broadcasts (reality becomes episodes)

Every location in KOOLA10 can trigger a broadcast.

============================================================

## 2. THE FOUR OFFICIAL SPIN-OFF SHOWS
============================================================

------------------------------------------------------------

### A. KOOLA10: AFTER HOURS (WEDNESDAYS)
------------------------------------------------------------
Format: Late-night surreal broadcast  
Location: KOOLA10 Diner  
Trigger: Every Wednesday at 3AM  
Host: The diner itself (sentient environment)

Structure:
- Cold Open: "The Diner Breathes"
- Diner Monologue
- Night Phenomenon (magical event)
- Guest Focus (KNB rider or local)
- Diner Judgment
- Reset

Tone: Neon, liminal, dreamlike, mythic.

------------------------------------------------------------

### B. IDIOMSZ x SKROLLZ (MAIN SHOW)
------------------------------------------------------------
Genre: Magical competition series  
Hosts:  
- Idiomsz (word-mage)  
- Skrollz (tech-mage)

Contestants: Anyone who enters the diner from the KNB station.

Mechanics:
- Portals = challenge worlds  
- Orbz = judges, helpers, saboteurs  
- Tickets = permission to leave the diner's influence  

------------------------------------------------------------

### C. KOOLA10: FREESTYLE OUTBACK
------------------------------------------------------------
Format: 3-on-3 magical streetball + WCT Tag  
Location: Behind the diner, next to the warehouse  
Trigger: When someone takes the trash out

Court Features:
- Shifting lines  
- Portal hazards  
- Orb referees  
- Streetlight modifiers  

Warehouse:
- KOOLA10 WCT Tag Arena  
- Moving crates, neon arrows, portals  

------------------------------------------------------------

### D. KOOLA10: SPIRAL CITY FREESTYLE PARK
------------------------------------------------------------
Format: BMX + Skate + Parkour freestyle battles  
Location: Spiral City Bike Park  
Trigger: When a battle breaks out

Zones:
- Neon Bowl  
- Graffiti Canyon  
- Infinity Rail  
- Loop of Fate  
- Rooftop Line  

Characters:
- IGI-Spin (BMX prodigy)  
- Kasso K10 (KOOLA10 skater)  
- Flip & Flare (parkour twins)  
- Taglord (graffiti mage)  
- Spokes (bike prophet)
